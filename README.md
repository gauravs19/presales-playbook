## The Graduation Model

The playbook uses a three-tier progression system:

- **Beginner** â€” No prior presales experience. Covers the "what" and the "how" of the SE role at a foundational level.
- **Mid-Level** â€” 1â€“3 years of SE experience. Covers deal strategy, advanced discovery, and commercial influence.
- **Senior** â€” 3+ years. Covers executive selling, team scaling, product influence, and leadership.

Each section is tagged with its level. Graduation checkpoints between tiers help you self-assess whether you're ready to progress.
