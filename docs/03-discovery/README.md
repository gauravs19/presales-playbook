# Discovery

## Overview
This chapter focuses on the discovery phase of the presales process, which is crucial for understanding customer needs and gathering relevant information.

### Key Topics
- Importance of Discovery
- Preparation for Discovery Calls
- Effective Questioning Techniques
- Stakeholder Mapping
- Pain Point Analysis
- Requirements Gathering

## Importance of Discovery
The discovery phase is essential for identifying customer pain points and understanding their requirements. It sets the foundation for the entire presales process.

## Preparation for Discovery Calls
Before engaging with customers, it's important to prepare thoroughly. This includes researching the customer, understanding their industry, and formulating relevant questions.

## Effective Questioning Techniques
Utilizing effective questioning techniques such as SPIN, MEDDIC, and BANT can help uncover valuable insights during discovery calls.

## Stakeholder Mapping
Identifying key stakeholders and understanding their roles in the decision-making process is critical for successful engagement.

## Pain Point Analysis
Analyzing customer pain points allows presales professionals to tailor solutions that address specific challenges.

## Requirements Gathering
Gathering technical requirements is essential for designing solutions that meet customer expectations. This section will outline best practices for effective requirements gathering.
