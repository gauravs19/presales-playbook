Stage 03 · Design

Solution Design Document

A deal-grade design: current state, target state, decision log, assumptions register — built to survive the architect who attacks it, the CFO who skims it, and the delivery team who inherits it.

PurposeA defensible technical blueprint that traces every choice back to a quantified pain
When to useAfter discovery is documented, before the first tailored demo; update at every scope change
OwnerSE / Solutions Architect
Time & output~45 min for v0.1 · to prospect tech team, champion, delivery review

How to use this document

  • The decision log is your armor. When their architect asks "why not event-driven?", the answer is already written — considered, rejected, reasons documented. Nothing builds trust faster than showing you rejected your own first idea.
  • The assumptions register is your insurance. Every unverified assumption gets a name and a date; when scope explodes in month four, the register proves it was flagged in month one.
  • Out-of-scope is a commercial clause. It's what the AE points at when the prospect asks for "one small addition." Write it as specifically as the inclusions.
  • Version on every change. A design that silently mutates across a nine-month cycle is a Frankenstein build in progress.
