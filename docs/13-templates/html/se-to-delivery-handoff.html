Stage 07 · Close

SE-to-Delivery Handoff

Everything promised, assumed, and red-flagged during the sale — captured before it evaporates, so delivery inherits a deal instead of a surprise. The close isn't the end of the deal; it's the start of the renewal.

PurposeTransfer nine months of deal knowledge — including the uncomfortable parts — while you still remember it
When to useWithin 5 business days of signature, before kickoff
OwnerSE · delivered in a live 45-min session, never just emailed
Time & output~30 min to write · to delivery lead, CSM, account team of record

How to use this document

  • Section 2 includes the implied promises. The contract says what was sold; the customer remembers what was said. Hand over both, with dates.
  • Section 3 is honesty, not confession. Every deal has demo-vs-production gaps. Documented, they're a configuration plan; discovered, they're an escalation with your name in the postmortem.
  • Section 6 is why this is a live session. Red flags are judgment, not data — they transfer in conversation, with questions.
  • Section 7 protects you. Bounded, written continuity is how you stay helpful without becoming the unpaid implementation engineer for two quarters.
