POC Plan & Success Criteria

The evaluation's contract: what will be proven, how success is measured, who does what, and what happens when it ends. An unstructured POC is free consulting with a deadline nobody agreed to.

PurposeLock criteria, scope, resources, and the commercial decision the POC feeds
When to useBefore ANY POC work begins — no signed plan, no sandbox access
OwnerSE, co-signed by the prospect's evaluation lead
Time & output~40 min draft + 1 negotiation meeting · to both sponsors, legal if paid

How to use this document

  • Three criteria, never ten. Ten criteria means nobody agreed what matters. Everything else is a demo topic, not an evaluation gate.
  • The decision link in section 1 is the whole document. A POC that ends in "we'll discuss internally" was never an evaluation — it was a free pilot.
  • Make their commitments as visible as yours. Named humans and hours turn "we couldn't find time to load the data" into a jointly owned slip.
  • The signature is the point. Unsigned, this is a wish list. Signed, it's what stops moving goalposts — new asks route through change control, cheerfully.
