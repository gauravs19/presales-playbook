Stage 02 · Discover

Discovery Summary

The one-page record of what was actually said — quantified pains, stakeholders, criteria, and the agreed proof path. Written while the quotes are still verbatim.

PurposeOwn the deal narrative; arm the demo team; get the prospect's confirmation in writing
When to useWithin 24 hours of every discovery call — no exceptions
OwnerSE
Time & output~25 min · external version to champion, full version to deal file

How to use this document

  • Two versions, one source. Sections 1, 4, 5, 7 go to your champion with "Did I capture this correctly?" — their corrections are free discovery; their silence is written confirmation. Sections 3 and 6 stay internal.
  • Verbatim quotes are the asset. "We lose about forty engineer-hours a month" survives a champion change, a procurement review, and a CFO challenge. Your paraphrase survives none of them.
  • A number in every pain row. A pain with no cost attached is a topic, not a pain — go back and quantify before you build anything.
  • One page. An executive absorbs it in ninety seconds; a demo engineer builds from it without a meeting. Longer means you transcribed instead of thinking.
