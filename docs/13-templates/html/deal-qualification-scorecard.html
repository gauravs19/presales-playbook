Stage 01 · Qualify

Deal Qualification Scorecard

A 15-minute, evidence-based go / no-go instrument for any new opportunity — before the first discovery call is booked.

PurposeForce an explicit pursue / challenge / walk decision with evidence attached
When to useWithin 48 hours of any new opportunity landing on your desk
OwnerSE, reviewed jointly with the AE
Time & output~15 min · goes to AE, SE manager, CRM record

How to use this document

  • Score what you know today, not what you hope to learn. Unknown = 1, never 3. A row full of 1s is not a bad deal — it's the agenda for your first discovery call.
  • The evidence column is mandatory. A score without evidence is a mood. One factual line per row, no adjectives.
  • Complete it with the AE, not to the AE. Present it as shared risk management: "here's where the deal is strong, here's what we must prove by the 15th."
  • Re-score at every stage gate. A deal qualified in January can rot by March; attach the delta to your weekly deal review.

Do

Don't
  • Score hope: "they seemed keen" is not evidence
  • Skip the blocker section because the deal is exciting
  • Average your way past a hard blocker

Before this scorecard ships

  • Every scored row has one factual evidence line with source and date
  • Blockers answered explicitly — not left blank
  • Reviewed live with the AE (not emailed cold)
