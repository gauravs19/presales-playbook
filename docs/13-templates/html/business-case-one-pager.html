Stage 06 · Commercial

Business Case One-Pager

The one page the CFO will actually read: cost of inaction, conservative benefits, honest TCO, payback — every number traceable to the prospect's own data or the signed POC.

PurposeMake the purchase an obviousness, not a risk, in under 90 seconds of reading
When to useEntering commercial negotiation — ideally alongside the Technical Win Report
OwnerSE (numbers) + AE (commercial framing)
Time & output~30 min if discovery was quantified · to economic buyer, finance, procurement

How to use this document

  • Their numbers or no numbers. Every figure carries a name, source, and date from discovery. Industry benchmarks make it a brochure; "as stated by your VP Eng on 12 March" makes it evidence.
  • Lead with the conservative case. Present the number you'd defend under hostile questioning; let the expected case be upside. One inflated input discounts the whole page — permanently.
  • Price their effort too. Including their internal hours in the TCO is what separates you from every vendor who conveniently forgot implementation cost.
  • Do the sensitivity math before they do. "Even if we're half wrong, payback is still 14 months" removes the last safe objection.
