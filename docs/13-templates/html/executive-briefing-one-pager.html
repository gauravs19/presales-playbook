Executive Briefing One-Pager

The pre-read that earns you the CxO meeting — decision-first, three honestly-priced options, the one diagram that matters, and an ask with a date. Frames the decision, not the product.

PurposeStart the executive conversation at decision altitude instead of climbing to it
When to use48 hours before any CxO meeting — briefing, steering review, final pitch
OwnerSE, pressure-tested by AE and champion
Time & output~20 min (business case exists) · to executives via champion; doubles as your talk track

How to use this document

  • Anchor to the initiative the executive already owns. "Reduces pipeline failures" is a manager's problem; "removes the scaling blocker on the initiative you committed to the board" is theirs.
  • Three options, honestly priced. A one-option pitch triggers sales-resistance; a decision structure makes the executive the decider — the only role they accept. Option 2 must be genuinely viable.
  • Name the risk before they do. Executives don't expect zero risk; they expect to not be surprised.
  • The ask has a date and a reason for the date — their calendar math, not your quarter-end.
