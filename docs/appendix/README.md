## Glossary
This section will define key terms and concepts used throughout the playbook to ensure clarity and understanding.

### Key Terms
- **Presales**: The activities and processes that occur before a sale is finalized, including customer engagement, solution design, and proposal development.
- **RFP**: Request for Proposal, a document issued by a buyer to solicit proposals from potential suppliers.
- **POC**: Proof of Concept, a demonstration to validate the feasibility of a proposed solution.
